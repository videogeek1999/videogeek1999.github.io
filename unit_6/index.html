About Jim

BMG was founded in 1999, by James Brooke. Prior to that, Mr. Brooke headed up the interactive division at GRA, an Atlanta training firm. While at GRA, Mr. Brooke learned the ins and outs of creating effective, visually stimulating multimedia courseware.

BMG continues that tradition of training excellence, utilizing state-of-the-art equipment during our productions so your videos look and sound their best!
